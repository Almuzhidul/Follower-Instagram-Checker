How to Get Your Instagram Followers Data

  1. Open Instagram settings
  2. Go to Accounts Center
  3. Select Your Information and Permissions
  4. Click Download Your Information
  5. Request your data and choose JSON format
  6. Download the ZIP file when Instagram sends it
  7. Upload the ZIP file below
