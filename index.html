How to Get Your Instagram Data

  1. Open Instagram Settings
  2. Go to Accounts Center
  3. Select Your Information and Permissions
  4. Click Download Your Information
  5. Choose JSON format
  6. Wait until Instagram sends the download link
  7. Download the ZIP file
  8. Upload the ZIP file below

Important: Your ZIP must contain:

  • followers_1.json
  • following.json
